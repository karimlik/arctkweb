Japanese Garden Project

info

The visualisation of "Japanese Garden" project was requested by "Azerinshaatlayihe" which is presented to "State Construction Safety Oversight Agency" division of Ministry of Emergency Situations.

Japanese Garden is planned to be constructed in Bayil district. This area is one of the highest points of Baku and located near the TV tower.

The Garden will be located in the zone of landslide, to prevent further demolition and harm to civilians.

 

Baku, Azerbaijan
Total area: 8000 m2
Year: 2019
