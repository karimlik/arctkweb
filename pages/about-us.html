about us

ArcTK Group is a leading architectural and construction company based in Baku, Azerbaijan. Since our establishment in 2015, we have been providing innovative solutions to clients across a wide range of industries. Our dedicated team of architects and engineers bring their expertise and passion for design to every project, ensuring that each one is completed to the highest standards.

At ArcTK Group, we believe in creating spaces that not only meet the functional needs of our clients, but also reflect their personalities and inspire their daily lives. Whether you're looking to build a new home, remodel your office, or undertake a large-scale commercial project, we have the skills and experience to bring your vision to life.

With a commitment to sustainability and a focus on using the latest construction technologies, ArcTK Group is well-positioned to help clients achieve their goals in a timely and cost-effective manner. Whether you're looking for a simple renovation or a complex new build, we have the team and resources to ensure your project is a success.

So if you're looking for a reliable and experienced architectural and construction partner, look no further than ArcTK Group. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help bring your vision to life.
